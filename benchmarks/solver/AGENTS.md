# Solver benchmarks

Read `README.md` before changing a protocol or interpreting results.

- Frozen protocols, consumed seeds, and retained result artifacts are immutable
  provenance, not reusable test inputs.
- New empirical claims require a versioned preregistered protocol, untouched
  seeds, an immutable pushed implementation revision, and complete retention of
  failures, caps, timeouts, and verifier-invalid rows.
- Development protocols are diagnostic evidence only. Synthetic and
  agent-generated replay profiles are not calibrated production flow.
