# Fees

Sybil uses a simple, transparent fee structure.

## Trading Fees

| Order Type | Fee |
|------------|-----|
| Regular orders | **5 bps** (0.05%) |
| Flash Quote orders | **5 bps** (0.05%) |
| Multi-market orders | **5 bps** (0.05%) |

<Info>
Fees are charged on the notional value of filled orders. Unfilled orders and cancellations are free.
</Info>

### Example

You buy 1000 YES shares at \$0.55:
- Notional: 1000 × \$0.55 = \$550
- Fee: \$550 × 0.05% = **\$0.275**

## Flash Quote Slot Auctions

Market makers who want Flash Quoting access must win a slot auction.

| Component | Cost |
|-----------|------|
| Slot auction | Market-determined (monthly) |
| Fill fees | Same as regular (5 bps) |

### Auction Details

- **Frequency**: Monthly
- **Slots available**: 10
- **Auction type**: Sealed-bid, second-price
- **Price paid**: (N+1)th highest bid

The slot auction is the cost of capital efficiency. Those who value it most will bid accordingly.

## Fee Distribution

Collected fees are distributed as follows:

```
Trading Fees (100%)
├── Protocol Treasury: 50%
├── Liquidity Incentives: 30%
└── Referrals & Partners: 20%
```

### Protocol Treasury

Funds protocol development, audits, and operations.

### Liquidity Incentives

Rewards for liquidity providers and early adopters. May include token distributions.

### Referrals & Partners

Revenue sharing with integrators, affiliates, and ecosystem partners.

| Competitor A | 10 bps | 5 bps rebate | Negotiated |

<Note>
Sybil doesn't distinguish maker/taker in batch auctions — everyone pays the same flat fee. This is simpler and fairer.
</Note>

## Gas Costs

Sybil is a validium. You don't pay gas for trading.

| Action | Gas Cost |
|--------|----------|
| Trading | **\$0** (off-chain) |
| Deposit | L1 gas (one-time) |
| Withdraw | L1 gas (one-time) |

Your trading activity happens off-chain. Only deposits and withdrawals touch L1.

## Yield Offset

Your collateral (sUSDS) earns yield while locked:

| APY | Monthly on \$10k |
|-----|-----------------|
| 4% | ~\$33 |
| 5% | ~\$42 |
| 6% | ~\$50 |

For active traders, yield often exceeds fees paid.
